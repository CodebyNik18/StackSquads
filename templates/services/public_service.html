Our Design Process

How we bring your brand vision to life

A collaborative, structured process that ensures every design we deliver is on-brand, on-time, and exactly what your business needs.

01

Discovery & Brand Brief

We start by getting deep into your brand — your values, audience, competitors, and goals. This brief becomes the foundation every design decision is built on.

02

Concept & Mood Boarding

We develop initial visual directions, style references, and mood boards so you can see the creative vision before a single pixel is placed. Your input shapes everything.

03

Design & Creation

Our designers get to work building your assets — with careful attention to hierarchy, colour psychology, typography, and every detail that makes great design great.

04

Review & Refinement

We present the designs, gather your feedback, and refine until every element is exactly right. We don't stop until you're genuinely proud of what we've created together.

05

Final Delivery & Brand Guide

We deliver all final files in every format you need — print-ready, web-optimised, and accompanied by a brand style guide so your team uses every asset correctly.

Common Questions

Creative & Graphic Design FAQ

How many revisions are included? ▾
All projects include multiple revision rounds — we don't cap revisions because we'd rather spend extra time getting it right than deliver something you're not completely happy with. We work with you until the design is exactly what you envisioned.
Will I own the final design files? ▾
Yes — upon final payment you receive full ownership of all deliverables, including editable source files (AI, PSD, Figma, etc.). Your brand assets are yours completely, with no licensing restrictions.
How long does a logo or brand identity project take? ▾
A logo project typically takes 1–2 weeks from brief to final delivery. A full brand identity system (logo, colours, typography, guidelines, and collateral) generally takes 3–5 weeks depending on scope and feedback turnaround times.
Can you match our existing brand if we just need new assets? ▾
Absolutely. If you already have an established brand identity, we can create new assets — social graphics, print materials, ads, or animations — that match your existing style perfectly. Just share your brand guide and existing files and we'll take it from there.
