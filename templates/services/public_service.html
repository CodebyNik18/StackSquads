Common Questions

Public Relations FAQ

What kind of publications can you get us featured in? ▾
It depends on your industry and story, but our network includes national outlets, industry-specific trade publications, regional news sites, and high-authority blogs. We target the publications that your specific audience reads and trusts.
How long does it take to get media coverage? ▾
Most clients start seeing placements within 4–8 weeks of starting the campaign. Some opportunities move faster, while top-tier national publications may take longer. PR is a relationship-driven process — results build over time.
Can you guarantee specific placements? ▾
We can't guarantee specific placements — no ethical PR firm can. However, our track record is strong because we only pursue placements where there's a genuine fit between your story and the publication's audience. We focus on quality over volume.
Does PR help with SEO? ▾
Absolutely. Every media placement typically includes a backlink from a high-authority domain — which directly improves your website's domain authority and search rankings. PR and SEO work together powerfully when done right.
