Common Questions

Google Ads FAQ

How much should I budget for Google Ads? ▾
There's no one-size-fits-all answer — it depends on your industry, competition, and goals. We work with budgets as low as $1,000/month for local businesses and scale up for competitive national campaigns. We'll recommend a realistic budget in your free consultation.
How quickly will I see results from Google Ads? ▾
Unlike SEO, Google Ads can drive traffic from day one. Most campaigns see meaningful results within the first 2–4 weeks as we gather data and optimize performance.
Do you manage the campaigns or just set them up? ▾
We provide full ongoing management — daily monitoring, weekly optimizations, A/B testing, and monthly reporting. We never set it and forget it.
Will I have access to my own Google Ads account? ▾
Absolutely. You own your account 100%. We operate as a manager under your account, giving you full transparency and the ability to view performance at any time.
