Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to build a website? ▾
Most websites take 3–6 weeks from kickoff to launch depending on complexity and scope. Simple landing pages can be done in 1–2 weeks, while larger e-commerce builds may take 8+ weeks.
Do you build on WordPress or custom code? ▾
We work with WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, Go High Level, and custom HTML/CSS/JS depending on your specific needs and budget. We'll recommend the best platform for your project.
Will I be able to update the site myself? ▾
Absolutely. We build with easy-to-use CMS platforms and provide a full walkthrough so you can manage your content independently. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages.
What do you need from me to get started? ▾
We'll need your branding assets (logo, colors, fonts), content direction, examples of sites you like, and your goals. Don't worry — we guide you through the entire process.
