Common Questions

Video Marketing FAQ

Do you handle filming or just editing? ▾
We offer both. We can handle full production including scripting, filming, and editing — or we can work with footage you already have and handle post-production only. We'll customize the service to your needs and budget.
What types of videos do you produce? ▾
We produce brand videos, product demos, testimonials, explainer videos, social content (Reels, TikToks, Shorts), YouTube ads, event recap videos, and more. If you can imagine it, we can produce it.
How long does video production take? ▾
Short-form social content can be turned around in 5–7 business days. Longer brand videos or ad campaigns typically take 2–4 weeks from brief to final delivery, depending on scope and revision rounds.
Do you also run YouTube ads with the videos you create? ▾
Yes — we offer full video ad management including campaign setup, audience targeting, bidding strategy, and ongoing optimization on YouTube and social platforms. Production + distribution under one roof.
