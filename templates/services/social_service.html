Common Questions

Social Marketing FAQ

Which platforms do you manage? ▾
We manage Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and Pinterest. We'll recommend the platforms that make the most sense for your specific audience and business type.
How much content do you create per month? ▾
It depends on the package, but typically we produce 12–20 posts per platform per month, including graphics, video content, and written captions — all scheduled and published for you.
Do I need to approve content before it's posted? ▾
Yes — we share a content calendar for your review and approval each month before anything goes live. Your brand voice and approval matter to us.
Can you run paid social ads alongside organic management? ▾
Absolutely. We offer combined organic + paid social packages that amplify your best content and drive measurable results with targeted ad campaigns on Meta, TikTok, and more.
